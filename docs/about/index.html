What Dark Horse Sports Is

Dark Horse Sports brings fast-moving sports debate, betting angles, and personality-driven conversation into one episodic feed.

The show is based in California, USA, and published as an episodic podcast under the Dark Horse Sports Talk banner.

What You Will Find Here

Each episode explores major sports stories, from soccer pressure moments to NBA, WNBA, and betting debates.

The archive is built for listeners who want a quick, sharp take on the stories shaping the week.

Voice and Style

The tone stays conversational and high energy, with the hosts bringing different angles across the table.

Rico, Mike, Ben, and Colin drive the conversation and keep the show moving with distinct perspectives.
